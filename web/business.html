I'm a local shop, how can I accept payments with Superlight?

Setup an account. Click on receive and type in the amount you are charging your customer. By scanning the QR-Code, the customer automatically pays you. The customer doesn't need Superlight, only some crypto wallet. But with Superlight, you both don't pay fees.

How much fees do I pay per customer payment?

Zero fees.

What are the costs for using Superlight as a payment gateway?

Zero costs.

Which cryptos can be used?

Every crypto currency.
But only Bitcoin and Stablecoins (USDC,Euroc) are free of network fees.

How do I send my accepted cryptos to my bank account.

You can use you cryptos directly with Superlight for further payments are payments for your employees.
If you want to payout to your bank account, you can do this manually or automatically (daily, weekly, monthly ...).

Our bank compatibility is built on Circle. They charge you 2.9% for payouts. Superlight doesn't charge you anything. We are building on it, to decrease or remove this fee. If you don't want to pay this fee, use your crypto directly without converting it to Eur/Usd.

How do I use Superlight?

In order to use Superlight. Please sign up for the early access. Then you will be notified as soon as the App is available on the iOs App Store.

Is Superlight an Exchange?

No.
Superlight is a non-custodial wallet. We do not hold any of your funds.

When will Superlight be available for the main-net

To test the infrastructure and stability of the app we will first launch on the test-net. We plan to launch on the main-net in Q4 2023.

When is lightning going to be supported?

After rollout of the main-net we will start working on lightning support. We plan to launch lightning support in Q2 2024.

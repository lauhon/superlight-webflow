How can I create a new wallet?

With each subwallet, you automatically create a new wallet. These subwallets are independently from each other and can't be connected from an outside viewer. Only the Key-Loss protection saves them all at once.

Where can I pay with Superlight?

Everywhere, where either cryptos or debit cards are accepted.
When you pay directly with cryptos, the seller saves up to 3% and may grant you a discount or cashback.

Why are the fees lower than with other wallets?

Because we want to ensure that all you do with money is yours and freely. Some fees we need to charge to keep our business running. You can checkout these fees completely transparent on our fees page.

Are you a bank?

No.
All funds are yours and we have no access to it. Only you have access.

Can I send cryptos directly to a Iban?

Yes you can!
When sending, just type in a Iban instead of an Wallet address and you are good to go. The sent cryptos will be automatically converted.
